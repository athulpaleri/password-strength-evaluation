##  What I Learned

- Password strength depends on **length**, **complexity**, and **randomness**
- Short or common passwords are easily cracked
- Strong passwords use **uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols**
- Online tools help evaluate password strength in real-time

---

##  Best Practices for Creating Strong Passwords

1. Use at least **12 characters**
2. Mix **uppercase + lowercase + numbers + symbols**
3. Avoid using **real names**, birth dates, or dictionary words
4. Use **passphrases** like `Red!Tiger123$Rain`
5. Use a **password manager** to store strong passwords
6. Enable **two-factor authentication (2FA)** whenever possible
